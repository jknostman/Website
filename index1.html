StagUp Showcase

The 5th annual StagUp Business Plan Competition will take place on April 5th, 2016 at 6 pm. This event is held at the Regina A. Quick Center for the Arts located on the campus of Fairfield University. Students of the University have joined their ideas together to form teams who will be competing against one another. These teams have made it past the qualifying rounds and will now be presenting their business plan to potential investors at this final event.
